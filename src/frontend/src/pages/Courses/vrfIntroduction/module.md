Randomness is essential for many types of applications. Think about how many games have randomness built-in. Rolling dice in dungeons and dragons, a random starting hand of poker, or a random next block in Tetris, randomness is an essential component of a majority of games. As we learned in Chainlink 101, smart contracts are a powerful tool for decentralized applications (dapps).
